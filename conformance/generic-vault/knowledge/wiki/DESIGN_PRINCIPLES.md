## Design Philosophy

Meridian Atelier builds furniture to last multiple generations. The studio's design language combines
structural clarity with material honesty: joinery is visible where it adds to the visual weight of
a piece, and materials are selected for how they age rather than how they look when new.

## Recurring Client Themes

Clients who commission Meridian pieces consistently prioritize:
- Longevity over trends.
- Natural and traceable materials over composites.
- Functional simplicity — pieces should do one thing superbly.
- Repairability — traditional joinery over adhesive-only construction.

## Relationship to Material Standards

The design principles are upstream of the material standards: if a design calls for a material that
does not appear on the approved list, the design must be revised before sourcing begins. Dana Okoro
has final say on material substitutions that conflict with the approved list.
